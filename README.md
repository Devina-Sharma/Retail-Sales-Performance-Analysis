# Superstore Sales Analysis

This project performs a comprehensive **exploratory data analysis (EDA)** of a retail Superstore sales dataset. It demonstrates skills in **data cleaning, feature engineering, data visualization, and business insights generation**.

# Dataset

- Contains sales orders for a retail store.
- Key columns: `Order Date`, `Ship Date`, `Category`, `Sub-Category`, `Sales`, `Profit`, `Customer Segment`, `Region`, `Product Name`.
- Downloaded from Kaggle: [Superstore Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets) *https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/konstantinognev/sample-superstorecsv*

# Tools & Libraries Used

- Python 3.x
- Pandas & NumPy (Data Manipulation)
- Matplotlib & Seaborn (Data Visualization)
- Jupyter Notebook (Analysis & Reporting)

# Project Highlights

- **Data Cleaning & Feature Engineering**: Removed duplicates, handled dates, calculated profit margin & shipping duration.
- **Sales & Profit Analysis**: By category, region, and customer segment.
- **Top Performers**: Identified top 10 products and customers by profit.
- **Discount & Profit Analysis**: Scatter plot to analyze discount effect on profitability.
- **Shipping Analysis**: Histogram of shipping durations to monitor delivery performance.
- **Correlation Analysis**: Heatmap to understand relationships between key numerical metrics.

# Key Insights

- Technology category generates the highest profit and margin.
- West region has the highest revenue Central region has lower margins.
- Consumer segment contributes most sales, Corporate segment has higher profitability.
- Discounts negatively impact profit, top products drive majority of revenue.
- Average shipping duration is ~3-4 days, delays can affect customer satisfaction.
